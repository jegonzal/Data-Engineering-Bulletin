The June, 2019 issue of the Data Engineering Bulletin is now available
electronically via the IEEE Computer Society Bulletin web site
http://tab.computer.org/tcde/bull_about.html. The ISSUES pages are
maintained both at the IEEE Computer Society
(http://sites.computer.org/debull/bull_issues.html) and at Microsoft
(ftp://ftp.research.microsoft.com/pub/debull/bull_issues.html).

Phil Berstein contributed an opinion piece that tells an interesting
story of transaction processing monitors running on middle-tier
servers, and predicts the return of middle-tier distributed
transactions to the mainstream after a 15-year decline.

Guoliang Li served as the assoiciate editor for this issue, which
features 6 articles on the interactions between database systems and
AI.
